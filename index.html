Welcome to AV Camper Conversions

Professional Camper Conversions in Glasgow & Scotland

We offer a bespoke camper conversion service to meet the needs of the individual, whether you want somewhere to brew up after a long day in the Highlands of Scotland, or spend a couple of weeks roaming the outer isles. With every campervan being designed from the floor up, we can custom fit the van conversion to meet your personal requirements.

Based in Lenzie, Glasgow, we serve customers throughout Scotland including Glasgow, Edinburgh, Stirling, and the surrounding areas. We can carry out anything from kitchen pod units to a basic day van to a full camper conversion and anything in-between.

Our Camper Conversion Services Include:

  • VW Transporter Conversions - T4, T5, T6, T7 specialist builds
  • Ford Transit Conversions - Custom, Connect, and full-size builds
  • MAN/VW Crafter Conversions - Large format van conversions for extended adventures
  • Kitchen Pod Units - Removable cooking solutions
  • Full Campervan Conversions - Complete builds with beds, kitchens, storage
  • Day Van Conversions - Basic setups for weekend adventures
  • Specialist Conversions - Bike vans, surf vans, wildlife hides

Only top quality components are used including Webasto heaters, Smev cooking equipment, Waeco fridges, and Vohringer furniture. Each project is designed to fit the base vehicle perfectly.

DIY Van Conversion Support

We can also supply all parts required if you fancy doing your own van conversion and can offer expert advice on how best to proceed. Perfect for those wanting to tackle their conversion project themselves with professional guidance.
